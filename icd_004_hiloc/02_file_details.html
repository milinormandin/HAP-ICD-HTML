File Details

HSAG maintains an electronic database known as “HILOC” for processing and adjudicating these requests and determinations, which contain pertinent information on beneficiaries’ functional status, existing social supports, and the type of care being recommended.HSAG conducts Level of Care (LOC) determinations on requests submitted by the Medicaid MCO health plans and community providers for Medicaid beneficiaries who require Long Term Supports and Services (LTSS) or those who are at risk of deteriorating to the nursing facility level of care.
