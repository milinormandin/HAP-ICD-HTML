Interface Overview

The KOLEA web portal is the State’s eligibility system, which serves as the point-of-entry for Medicaid benefits in Hawaii and captures data such as demographic information, income, and beneficiary assets and resources. Once a Medicaid eligibility determination has been completed, KOLEA provides real-time feedback of the results to the applicant, as well as passing the eligibility results over to the HPMMIS. The HPMMIS returns enrollment data back to KOLEA for each beneficiary who is approved. All information on applicants and approved beneficiaries, including the unique identifier given to all enrollees, is currently retained by KOLEA.
