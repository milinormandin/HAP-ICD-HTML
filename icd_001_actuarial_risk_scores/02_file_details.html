File Details

Dual-eligible members are excluded. These members are not risk adjusted since the majority of their medical expenses are paid by Medicare. All other members are included regardless of how many months of exposure they have in the base year. For risk adjustment purposes we only include members with at least 6 months of exposure. The months of exposure in the base year is included as a field for reference. Health plan assignment reflects June 2023 enrollment for snapshot enrolled members. For non-snapshot members it is assigned based on the member’s last month of eligibility in the base year.
